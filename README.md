## Milestone 5 Summary  
Milestone 5 focuses on consolidating deliverables from Cohort 2.  
Key objectives include:  

- Evaluation and reporting of Cohort 2 outcomes.  
- Finalization of debugging and testing across core modules.  
- Collection and analysis of participant and stakeholder feedback.  
- Onboarding and preparation of Cohort 2 participants.  
- Submission of updated technical documentation and impact frameworks.
